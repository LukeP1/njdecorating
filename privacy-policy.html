Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how N J Broxholme collects, uses and protects personal information provided by visitors to this website.

1. Who we are

N J Broxholme Ltd is a painting and decorating contractor based in Worksop, England. If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact us using the details on our Contact page.

2. Information we collect

The main way we collect personal information is when you contact us directly, for example by phone or email, or by completing a form and providing your details. This may include your name, contact details and any information you choose to provide about your project.

3. How we use your information

We use the personal information you provide to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide quotes;
  • Arrange and carry out our painting and decorating services;
  • Keep basic records of work we have carried out for you.

4. Cookies and tracking

This website does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies. We do not use analytics cookies, advertising cookies or any other form of online tracking on this site.

5. Sharing your information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information where it is necessary to deliver our services (for example, with trusted subcontractors) or where we are required to do so by law.

6. How we protect your information

We take reasonable steps to keep your personal information secure and to prevent unauthorised access, use or disclosure.

7. How long we keep your information

We keep personal information only for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry, provide our services and meet any legal or accounting obligations.

8. Your rights

Depending on applicable data protection law, you may have rights in relation to your personal data, such as the right to access, correct or request deletion of the information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details on our Contact page.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the updated policy will apply from the date it is published.
